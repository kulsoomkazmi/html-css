Part 1

My favourite food is Biryani.Yes,I know how to make it

Ingredients

  • 3 large Onions
  • 1 kg Rice
  • 8 Tomatos
  • oil as per your need
  • salt as per your taste
  • Shan masala packet
  • Corriander
  • mutton/beef/chicken

Steps:

  1. First you fry an cutt onions in heated oil until it turns brown golden.
  2. Then add cutt tomatoes and fry until it soggies.
  3. Then add meat,masala packet,salt.Bhonify it.
  4. In a separate pot boil rice.
  5. In the end layering your rice on top of gravy and let it on low flame for 5 minutes.
  6. serve it with salad and yogurt
